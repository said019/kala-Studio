# Kala Barre Studio Product Context

## Register

brand

## Product Purpose

Kala Barre Studio is a boutique barre studio platform for reservations, payments, memberships, attendance, wallet passes and community progress in San Luis Potosi. The public landing page must sell the studio experience, while the logged-in app helps students reserve classes and track commitment.

## Users

- New students evaluating whether Kala feels approachable, energetic and easy to join.
- Returning students who buy packages, reserve classes and track their progress.
- Studio staff who validate payments, manage schedules and mark check-ins.

## Brand

Kala should feel close, casual, feminine, energetic and polished. The visual world is warm white, berry wine, coral, olive and orange, supported by real class photos and Instagram-inspired studio imagery.

## Strategic Principles

- Show the actual studio rhythm instead of abstract wellness claims.
- Make progress feel visible: assistance, effort and community should be easy to understand.
- Keep copy short and direct. Spanish UI should sound natural for the studio audience.
- Avoid generic gym aggression. Kala is motivating, warm and focused.

## Anti-References

- Dark neon fitness apps.
- Corporate SaaS dashboards on the landing page.
- Generic three-card feature grids with icons only.
- Overly technical explanations in public-facing sections.
